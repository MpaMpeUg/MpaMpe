Target

Our main target is to reach out to the future founders, those with big ideas, great entrepreneurhip insight and bring them alive through a network and a pool of willing investors.

MpaMpe is a platform with cutting edge features and invaluable resources for African young entrepreneurs, an arsenal to the dreamers just like ourselves, a way to breathe life into a seemingly impossible project.

For many entrepreneurs, securing funding is vital to getting their business up and running. However, today’s mostly-remote business climate means that traditional in-person methods may not always be feasible.

As Businesses are fighting to get noticed by the greatest investors and have their work appreciated,at the same time, many investors are frustrated and not sure who will have the greatest return on investments on the possible options, nor are they sure of any business's market capitalization. Well, you don't have to struggle to go through that with MpaMpe .

MpaMpe will help entrepreneurs, small-scale business owners, artists, musicians, filmmakers, designers, and other creators find the resources and support they need to make their ideas a reality. We believe, tens of thousands of creative projects — big and small — have come to life with the support of the MpaMpe community.

Our Mission

Our Mission Is To Help Bring Creative Projects To Life Through Digital Financing

MpaMpe aims to provide a fast way to raise funds with no upfront fees. We also aim at providing investors or backers with a way to track the progress of the entrepreneurs.
